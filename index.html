Personal Biography:

Born and raised in Downey, CA, I graduated from Warren High School in 2016 and promptly started my undergraduate studies at the University of California - Riverside pursuing a B.S. degree in Biology with pre-med aspirations. From January 2016 to August 2017 I was employed with the City of Downey's Parks and Recreation Department as a Recreation Leader. My duties included: monitoring city grounds, enforcing city codes and ordinances, preparing and disassembling the reservation hall, assisting at city-held events, and acting as a point of reference for community members. Following that job, I was then employed as a Security Guard for Disneyland Resort in sunny Anaheim, CA from August 2017 to September 2018. With this position I greeted and screened all guests and their belongings entering the resort. Additionally, I used behavior assessment techniques and area patrolling to ensure a safe environment for guests and their families. After working at Disneyland I accepted an offer as a Salesman at my local Best Buy in Downey, CA; specifically in the Home Theater department with a specialization in LG tv's. I started working at Best Buy in October 2018 and my last day will be in December 2019. Currently I am a Bartender and Server at Benihana in Downey, CA.

As I prepare to graduate from UC Riverside this June, I am also studying to take the MCAT exam so that I can apply to medical schools this summer as well. I believe my previous job occupations have strengthened my communication and interpersonal skills and will allow me to communicate and relate with my future patients effectively and efficiently when I become a physician.
